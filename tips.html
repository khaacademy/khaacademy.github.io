\(\text{Last-Minute Tips}\)

Derivative / Integral Cheat Sheet: Link


\(\underline{\text{Units}}\) - Put units when needed.
\(\underline{\text{Absolute Extrema}}\) - When finding absolute extrema, check endpoints.
\(\underline{\text{Critical Points/Value}}\) - Critical points are points \((a,b)\), while critical values are values \((x=a)\).
\(\underline{\text{EVT/MVT/IVT}}\) - Check conditions. Endpoints do not count toward these theorems.
\(\underline{\text{Area vs. Integral}}\) - Area is always positive, as opposed to integrals. \((\text{e.g.} \int_{-\frac{\pi}{2}}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sin xdx\neq\text{area of }\sin x\text{ from }-\frac{\pi}{2}\text{ to }\frac{\pi}{2})\)
\(\underline{\text{U-Substitution}}\) - Change bounds or substitute at end.
\(\underline{\text{Quotient Rule}}\) - Pneumonic \(\Rightarrow\) (Low D High minus High D Low, Over Square of Below)
\(\underline{\text{L'Hôpital's}}\) - Only when indeterminate \((\text{e.g. }\frac{0}{0})\). Differentiate top and bottom separately.
\(\underline{\text{Integrating 1/x}}\) - \(\int\frac{1}{x}=\ln |x|\). Do not drop the absolute value.
\(\underline{\text{End of Test Skim}}\) - Upon finising a test, I recommend a 10 second skim of the whole test before turning it in. See if any questions required units / asked for a point instead of coordinate / other potential sources of silly mistakes.
