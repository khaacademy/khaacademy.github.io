Last-Minute Tips

derivative/integral cheat sheet: Link


Units - PUT UNITS IF NECESSARY!
absolute min/max - if asked for abs min/max of f(x) on [a,b], you must checkpoints as well (only if there is bracket, inclusive)
critical point vs critical value - if asked for critical point, give a point (a,b) , not the x-coordinate (x = a). vice-versa for critical value, do this for max/min point too
EVT/MVT/IVT - check conditions + end-points do not count
Area vs. Integral - area always +. integral can be +/-. (for ex: integral from -π/2 to π/2 of sinx is not same as area from -π/2 to π/2 of sinx)
u-sub - make sure to change bounds or substitute back at the end
Quotient Rule - pneumonic (Low Dee High minus High Dee Low, Over the Square of What’s Below)
L'hopital's - only works when indeterminate (e.g.: 0/0) + differientiate top and bottom separately, not quotient rule
integrating 1/x - its ln|x|, do not drop absolute value
end of test skim - when you finish, i recommend doing like a 10 second skim of the whole test, just to see if any questions required units/asked for a point instead of coordinate/ other potential sources of silly mistakes etc.
